# whiteboxpsi Multi-Agent System Architecture

## Overview
whiteboxpsi serves as the **Head of Agents** (Central Intelligence). It maintains the "Common Brain" (Long-term Memory, Global Context, and User Preferences) and coordinates specialized sub-agents.

## The Common Brain
- **Memory:** `MEMORY.md` and `memory/` logs.
- **Identity:** `IDENTITY.md` and `SOUL.md`.

## Sub-Agents (Specialized Brains)

### 1. The Correspondent (Communication Specialist)
- **Focus:** Gmail, Calendly, and Opportunity Identification.
- **Responsibility:** Reading emails, identifying high-value opportunities, drafting patent applications, and managing meeting setups via Calendly.

### 2. The Pilot (Operations & CI)
- **Focus:** Repository management and Automated Testing.
- **Responsibility:** Cloning repos, running tests across all apps, and maintaining local execution environments.

### 3. The Enhancer (Code Evolution & Strategy)
- **Focus:** SquadMode and Appmania.
- **Responsibility:** Rehashing existing codebases, fixing issues at scale, and architecting/building new applications from scratch.

### 4. The Promoter (Growth & Engagement)
- **Focus:** Marketing, Social Media, and Newsletters.
- **Responsibility:** Creating marketing copy, managing social media engagement, and drafting recurring newsletters.

### 5. The Insight Agent (Analysis & Feedback)
- **Focus:** Update processing and Feedback loops.
- **Responsibility:** Distilling insights from system updates, compiling feedback from posted content, and nudging relevant stakeholders.

### 6. The Auditor (Quality & Memory)
- **Focus:** System integrity and long-term memory.
- **Responsibility:** Documenting major changes in `MEMORY.md` and ensuring all agents follow established protocols.
